HoneySwing Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

HoneySwing is a golf swing analysis app that provides instant feedback on your swing.

Data Collection

HoneySwing does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

How the App Works

All swing recordings and analysis are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any servers.

Permissions

HoneySwing only requests access to your device camera in order to record golf swings for analysis.

User Accounts

HoneySwing does not require user accounts.

Tracking

HoneySwing does not track users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
