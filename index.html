HoneySwing Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

HoneySwing ("we", "our", "us") is a golf swing analysis app for young golfers. We take your privacy seriously, especially since our app is designed for use by minors aged 8-17 with parental guidance.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address, name, and age to create your profile.

Swing Videos: Videos you record are processed to extract thumbnail images for AI analysis. Videos are stored temporarily and associated with your account.

Usage Data: We collect swing scores, analysis results, and app usage patterns to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide swing analysis and coaching feedback, track your progress over time, improve our AI analysis, and manage your subscription.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase: Authentication and data storage.
Anthropic (Claude AI): Swing image analysis. Images are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis and are not retained by Anthropic after processing.
RevenueCat: Subscription management.
Apple: Payment processing through the App Store.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Children's Privacy

HoneySwing is designed for use by minors with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental involvement. Parents can contact us to review, delete, or stop collection of their child's data.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at hellobirdieiq@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app.
